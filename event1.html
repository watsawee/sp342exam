Meringue or meringue (French: meringue, English: Meringue) is a type of Swiss and French dessert.
Made from egg whites that are beaten with powdered sugar until fluffy.
and adding molding agents such as cream of tartar, corn starch, etc. In some recipes,
vanilla, almond, and coconut flavors are often added as well. It is light,
fluffy and extremely sweet. Its well-known name is meringue cookies.
